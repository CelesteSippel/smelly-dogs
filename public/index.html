The Top Three Smelliest Dogs!

The three most smelliest dogs are the Pug, the Bulldog and the Basset Hound.

Pug

Pugs are known for their gassiness and bad breath. They also have lots of wrinkeles that need to be cleaned often to lessen their odor.

Bulldog

The bulldog is known for poor teeth alignment which can lead to large amounts of plaque and tooth decay. Bulldogs also have tons of wrinkles that get smelly without proper care.

Basset Hound

Basset Hounds have long ears that are prone to infection which leads to a lot of foul odor. Plus those long ears rub on the ground and pick up all the smells from the ground.
